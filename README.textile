h1. Laterstars for Safari

Just a simple Safari extension to replace the one-click fave bookmarklet from Laterstars

h2. Download

You can download Laterstars for Safari *"here":http://epogue.s3.amazonaws.com/laterstars-01.safariextz*

h2. Credit

Pretty much all the credit goes to the Laterstars team. I just adapted their incredible bookmarklet into Safari extension form.
